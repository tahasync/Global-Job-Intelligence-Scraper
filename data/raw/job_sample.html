JiBe is a cloud-based, fully integrated ERP system designed specifically for the shipping industry. Our platform helps shipping companies enhance productivity, efficiency, and safety while significantly reducing operational costs. By enabling increased automation and streamlining workflows, JiBe minimizes reliance on email and paper, allowing organizations to focus on what matters most. We are currently seeking a highly skilled engineer to join our dynamic team and contribute directly to the growth and success of our product.

Key Responsibilities
- Design, develop, and maintain scalable and reliable software solutions within the JiBe ERP system.
- Collaborate closely with cross-functional teams including product management, QA, and customer support to deliver high-quality features.
- Participate in code reviews, testing, and continuous integration to ensure robust and maintainable code.
- Identify and troubleshoot performance bottlenecks and bugs to improve system stability and user experience.
- Contribute to the design and implementation of new workflows and automation features that reduce manual processes.
- Stay up-to-date with emerging technologies and industry trends to continuously enhance the product.
- Support deployment and post-release monitoring to ensure smooth operation in production environments.

Required Qualifications
- Proven experience in software engineering, preferably with cloud-based ERP or SaaS platforms.
- Strong proficiency in relevant programming languages and frameworks used in JiBe’s technology stack.
- Solid understanding of software development lifecycle, agile methodologies, and best coding practices.
- Experience with database design, optimization, and working with both SQL and NoSQL databases.
- Ability to write clean, maintainable, and well-documented code.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Strong communication skills and ability to work effectively within a team environment.

Preferred Qualifications and Benefits
- Experience in the shipping or logistics industry is a plus.
- Familiarity with cloud infrastructure and DevOps practices.
- Exposure to automation tools and workflow management systems.
- Opportunity to work in a fast-growing company with a collaborative and innovative culture.
- Chance to make a direct impact on product development and company growth.

JiBe offers a challenging and rewarding environment for engineers who are passionate about building impactful software solutions. Join us to be part of a team that is transforming the shipping industry through technology.
